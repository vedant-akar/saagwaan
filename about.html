Who We Are

Born in Jaipur.
Built by Hand.

SAAGWAAN is a luxury furniture and furnishing brand rooted in Jaipur, Rajasthan — a city with centuries of craft in its bones. We design and manufacture bespoke, made-to-order solid wood furniture and premium interior furnishings that speak to a refined sensibility.

Every piece leaves our workshop bearing the mark of deliberate craft — no shortcuts, no mass production. We believe a home deserves furniture with a soul.
